The app is free to use. AdMob banners appear in non‑intrusive areas to support ongoing development.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection: Prime Walk Athlete does not collect personally identifiable information. All step data accessed via Apple HealthKit remains on your device.

Health Data: Step count information is read from HealthKit solely to calculate in‑app statistics and achievements. We never write data back to HealthKit.

Advertising: The app displays Google AdMob banner ads. AdMob may use device identifiers (such as the advertising ID) to serve personalized ads in accordance with Google AdMob policies.

Offline Operation: The core features—including step tracking, prime calculations, and badge generation—function without an internet connection.
